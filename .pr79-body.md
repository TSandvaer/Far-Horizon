2. **Flicker fix (`86caayvfz`):** the 1st-pass band strategy (push `shadowDistance` to 360u on 4 cascades) cleared the band but the Sponsor STILL saw shimmer (build `a5282c6`: "still present, maybe too aggressive") — 360u stretches even 4 cascades thin in the FAR field (~5 texels/unit). ## Trace diagnosis — flicker fix (`86caayvfz`)

**Confirmed root cause: single-cascade shadow-map stretch at the near field, then far-field stretch at 360u.** Texel-density math from the committed URP params:

| build | shadowDistance | cascades | map | near-field texels/unit |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| URP default | 50 | 1 | 2048 | ~18 |
| prior shipped (no flicker) | 160 | 1 | 2048 | ~9 |
| flagged `adde6b0` | 360 | 1 | 2048 | **~4** ← shimmer |
| 1st pass (band-by-distance) | 360 | 4 | 2048 | ~85 near, but **~5 FAR** ← shimmer persisted |
| **this fix (shipped)** | **220** | **4** | **4096** | dense across all cascades; soft filter kills crawl percept |

The 1st-pass 360u edit recovered near-field density with 4 cascades but the FAR cascades (spanning ~168u..360u) still sat at only ~5 texels/unit, so mid/far shadow edges crawled regardless of near-cascade density. More cascades cannot fix the far field at 360u — the budget is spread over too much world. The shipped fix instead backs the distance to 220u (1.6× denser per cascade everywhere), fades the boundary with a wide cascade-border in the far distance where the Exp2 fog already hides it, and adds a 7×7 soft-shadow tent filter that blurs the edge across texels so per-frame texel-quantization stops reading as flicker.

**Alternatives ruled out (not just deprioritized):**
- **Bloom/exposure instability** — REFUTED. Post stack is static: `Bloom.intensity 0.25` / `threshold 1.02`, no `AutoExposure` component anywhere, `ColorAdjustments.postExposure` a flat `0.0` override. Nothing in the stack pulses frame-to-frame under static daylight lighting (`QualityPassGen.cs`).
- **Real frame drops** — REFUTED. The render-setting edits carry zero per-frame CPU/GPU cost change that could cause frame-time spikes. The Sponsor's phrasing ("SEEM like lagging") describes a visual shimmer, not stutter.

## Exact settings changed

`BootstrapProject.ConfigureUrp()` + `Assets/Settings/FarHorizonURP.asset` (kept consistent — committed values):
- `m_ShadowDistance`: **160 → 220** (covers the framed grass + roam reach; backed OFF from the 1st-pass 360u that re-opened far-field crawl)
- `m_CascadeBorder`: **0.35 → 0.5** (wider soft fade — the boundary now fades GENTLY in the far distance instead of reading as a hard band)
- `m_ShadowCascadeCount`: **1 → 4** (near-field texel density; `m_Cascade4Split` = `0.067 / 0.2 / 0.467`, URP 4-cascade default)
- `m_SoftShadowsSupported`: **0 → 1** (soft/filtered shadows ON — the 7×7 tent filter is the direct flicker kill the cascade work alone could not deliver)
- `m_MainLightShadowmapResolution`: **2048 → 4096** (doubles linear texel density across ALL cascades, backing up the distance-reduction + soft filter)

## Perf note

4 shadow-map render passes vs 1 on the single directional light, at 4096 vs 2048. Negligible on this world: ONE sun, no additional-light shadows (`m_AdditionalLightShadowsSupported: 0`), static low-poly geometry. No GC/per-frame-alloc impact (config-only).
