## Silent-killer seams to guard (carry-forward + new)

1. **Surface-parity drift (HUD contract).** The whole point is the HUD binds hunger IDENTICALLY to warmth. A subtly-different surface (`Current01` returning 0..max instead of 0..1; `IsCritical` polled instead of derived; `Changed` passing nothing instead of `Current01`) green-passes the need's own tests but breaks `86caamkxv`. **Guard:** an explicit surface-shape assertion + the vocabulary-contract grep against the thirst sibling.
2. **`Changed` not firing on a real value change** — the subscribe-never-poll HUD freezes silently. Mirror `WarmthNeed`'s `Changed_Fires_OnDecayAndSatisfaction` AND `Changed_DoesNotFire_OnNoOpChange` (over-firing on no-ops is its own bug — wasted HUD repaints / pulse re-triggers).
3. **Eat seam half-wiring** (cross-lane with `86caa5zz3`). Berry consumed but no restore, or restore with no consume. **Guard:** the atomic integration test + an explicit owner for that test (this ticket OR `86caa5zz3`, cited — not assumed).
4. **deltaTime≈0 headless false-green.** Decay that integrates per-frame green-passes EditMode and never ticks in the build. **Guard:** the PlayMode real-window test (non-negotiable).
5. **Floor overshoot.** A long Update gap (one big `TickSeconds`) must clamp to the floor, not blow past to 0.
