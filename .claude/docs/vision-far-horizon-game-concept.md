Far Horizon is a survival game. The audience is children as well as adults, it is important that the difficulty level / Scariness can be adjusted in the players game settings.

A young man is shipwrecked in the middle of the ocean and washes up on an mysterious unexplored island. 

Here the young man picks up wooden branches and small stones on the ground and builds a crafting table using only wood.

He can now build his first axe using wood and stone.

He can now use the axe to chop wood to get wood planks faster. 

He builds a bonfire out of stone and wood and lights it.

How he is dries up at warm bonfire feeling safer having an axe, but he starts to get hungry. He searches for bushes with berries and harvest some to give small satisfaction to his hunger. 

The young man begins to get thirsty after eating the berries and goes look for a pond of fresh water. He finds a small pond and drinks with his hand, satisifying small amount of thirst with each scoop. (later player can craft a cup or something else to hold more water)
